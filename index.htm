Who Am I?

I'm Eyob Ayalew, a self-taught web developer, researcher, and entrepreneur from Ethiopia. I thrive at the intersection of technology, culture, and innovation, building digital solutions that make an impact.

I founded Inspire Tech Hub (ITH), a startup delivering tech solutions to Ethiopian organizations and global clients. From multilingual web applications to SaaS platforms, I specialize in web development, cybersecurity, and software engineering, working with TypeScript, Laravel, Flutter, Java, and Django.

Beyond coding, I’m passionate about cultural preservation, education, and digital accessibility. I’ve led localization projects, researched Ethiopian history and cuisine, and contributed to youth empowerment and climate awareness.

Whether developing software, managing open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring developers, I believe in technology as a force for change. Let’s connect and innovate together!
